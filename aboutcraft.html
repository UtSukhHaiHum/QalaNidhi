A Timeless Tale of Tradition

Odisha's paper mache masks, or 'Mukha', are more than just vibrant faces. They're a narrative woven from eco-friendly materials, steeped in cultural heritage, and pulsating with the artistic brilliance of Odia artisans.

These masks hold a mirror to Odisha's soul, reflecting stories from folklore, epics, and everyday life. From depicting deities to portraying theatrical characters, each mask is a unique masterpiece.

About Raghurajpur Paper Mache Masks

Raghurajpur, a heritage village in Odisha, is renowned for its exquisite handcrafted paper mache masks. Learn about the rich cultural heritage and craftsmanship behind these masks.

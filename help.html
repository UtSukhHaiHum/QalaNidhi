Frequently Asked Questions

Ready-Made Masks

To place an order, simply browse our selection of ready-made masks, select the one you like, choose the quantity, and click ‘Add to Cart’. Proceed to checkout, fill in your shipping details, and complete the payment process.

We accept a variety of payment methods including credit/debit cards, net banking, and popular e-wallets.

Orders can be canceled or modified within 24 hours of placement. Please contact our support team immediately for assistance.

Custom Masks

For a custom mask, visit our ‘Custom Order’ page, provide the details of your design, and submit your request. Our team will reach out to you to confirm the design and proceed with the creation process.

You can customize the size, color palette, and design elements. Share your ideas, and we’ll bring them to life!

Custom masks typically take 4-6 weeks to create and deliver, depending on the complexity of the design.

Common Issues and Solutions

If you’re having trouble signing up or logging in, try resetting your password using the ‘Forgot Password’ link. If the problem persists, contact our support team.

Follow the step-by-step guide on our ‘How to Order’ page. If you encounter any issues, please reach out to our customer service for assistance.

Check our ‘Shipping Policy’ page for estimated delivery times. Use the tracking number provided to monitor your order’s journey. Contact us if there’s an unexpected delay.

Visit our ‘Returns & Refunds’ page for detailed instructions on initiating a return. Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days after we receive the returned item.

Troubleshooting Guides

If your payment didn’t go through, check for alerts from your bank, ensure your card details are correct, and that you have sufficient funds. Still need help? Contact us.

Clear your browser cache and cookies if you’re experiencing technical difficulties. If issues continue, try using a different browser or device, or get in touch with our support team.
